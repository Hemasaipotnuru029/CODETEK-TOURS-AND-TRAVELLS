Cancellation Policy

1. Introduction

We aim to offer the best travel experience possible, but we understand that sometimes plans change. This policy outlines the conditions and processes for cancelling bookings with our company.

2. General Conditions

All cancellations must be made in writing (via email or our online contact form). The date of the cancellation request will be the date on which we receive your written notice.

  • All cancellation requests must be submitted in writing (via email or our online contact form).
  • The date of the cancellation request will be considered the date we receive your written notice.
  • Refunds will be processed using the same payment method used for the original booking.
  • Refunds will be issued according to the terms outlined below.

3. Cancellation by the Customer

If you need to cancel your booking, the following terms will apply:

  • More than 30 days before departure: Full refund minus any non-refundable deposits or fees.
  • 15-30 days before departure: 50% refund of the total booking cost.
  • 7-14 days before departure: 25% refund of the total booking cost.
  • Less than 7 days before departure: No refund.

4. Cancellation by the Company

In rare cases, we may need to cancel a tour or service due to unforeseen circumstances. If this happens, you will be entitled to a full refund or the option to reschedule.

5. Non-Refundable Items

The following items are non-refundable:

  • Booking deposits
  • Travel insurance
  • Special request fees
  • Additional service charges (e.g., upgrades, customizations)

6. Force Majeure

In the event of force majeure (e.g., natural disasters, political instability, pandemics), refunds will be considered on a case-by-case basis. We may offer rescheduling or credit towards future bookings as an alternative to refunds.

7. Refund Processing Time

Refunds will be processed within 14 business days of receiving a valid cancellation request. The time it takes for the refund to appear in your account may vary depending on your bank or credit card issuer.
