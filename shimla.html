Shimla 2N Sighitseeing


  • Day 1: Arrival and Local Exploration
    Morning: Arrive in Shimla: Check into your hotel and freshen up. Mall Road: Start your exploration with a leisurely walk along Mall Road. It's the main street in Shimla, bustling with shops, cafes, and colonial architecture.
    Afternoon: Christ Church: Visit Christ Church, one of the oldest churches in North India, located at the end of Mall Road. Scandal Point: Stroll to Scandal Point, a popular meeting place with a fascinating history and great views of the surrounding hills.
    Evening: The Ridge: Spend the evening at The Ridge, an open space that offers panoramic views of the mountains. It's also a cultural hub where events and festivals are often held.
    Dinner: Enjoy dinner at one of the restaurants on Mall Road or the Ridge, with options ranging from local Himachali cuisine to international dishes

  • Day 2: Excursions and Nature
    Morning: Kufri Excursion: Head to Kufri, about 16 km from Shimla, known for its scenic beauty and adventure activities. You can visit the Kufri Fun World or enjoy horse riding and panoramic views from Mahasu Peak. Green Valley: : On your way back, stop at Green Valley, a picturesque spot offering lush greenery and serene views, perfect for photography.
    Afternoon: Jakhoo Temple:Visit Jakhoo Temple, dedicated to Lord Hanuman, located on Jakhoo Hill. The temple offers stunning views of the city and surrounding mountains. Be cautious of the monkeys! Evening: Viceregal Lodge (Indian Institute of Advanced Study):Explore this historic building, which was once the residence of the British Viceroy. The architecture and gardens are splendid.
    dinner:Choose a cozy spot for your dinner. Consider trying Himachali dishes like Chana Madra, Siddu, or Babru
  • Day 3: Arrival and Local Exploration
    Morning:Relax and Pack: Enjoy a leisurely breakfast and take in the views one last time before packing. Depart from Shimla: Check out from your hotel and head to your next destination.
