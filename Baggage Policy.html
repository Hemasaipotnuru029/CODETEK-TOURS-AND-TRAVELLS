Baggage Policy

1. Introduction

We aim to ensure a smooth and enjoyable travel experience for all our customers. This baggage policy outlines the conditions and guidelines for baggage allowance, restrictions, and handling during your tour.

2. General Conditions

All customers must adhere to the baggage guidelines outlined in this policy. Failure to comply may result in additional charges or the refusal of certain items on our tours.

3. Baggage Allowance

Each customer is allowed the following baggage:

  • Checked Baggage: One piece of checked baggage up to 20kg (44 lbs).
  • Carry-On Baggage: One piece of carry-on baggage up to 7kg (15 lbs).
  • Dimensions for checked baggage should not exceed 158 cm (62 inches) in total (length + width + height).
  • Dimensions for carry-on baggage should not exceed 55 cm x 40 cm x 20 cm (22 inches x 16 inches x 8 inches).

4. Excess Baggage

If your baggage exceeds the allowed weight or size, the following conditions will apply:

  • Excess baggage fees will be charged at the rate of $10 per kg (2.2 lbs) for checked baggage.
  • Oversized baggage fees will be charged at the rate of $50 per piece for checked baggage that exceeds the size limits.
  • Carry-on baggage that exceeds the weight or size limits will need to be checked in, and excess baggage fees will apply.

5. Prohibited Items

The following items are strictly prohibited in both checked and carry-on baggage:

  • Explosives, fireworks, and flammable materials
  • Compressed gases (including aerosols)
  • Corrosive substances (e.g., acids, alkalis)
  • Firearms and ammunition
  • Sharp objects (e.g., knives, scissors)
  • Radioactive materials
  • Any other items prohibited by local or international regulations

6. Special Baggage

For special baggage items such as sports equipment, musical instruments, or oversized items, please contact us in advance. Special arrangements may be required, and additional fees may apply.

7. Lost or Damaged Baggage

In the event that your baggage is lost or damaged during the tour, please report it to our staff immediately. We will assist in locating lost baggage and processing claims for damaged items. Compensation will be provided in accordance with applicable regulations and our company policy.
