Refund Policy

1. Introduction

We strive to provide the best possible experience for our customers. However, we understand that there may be circumstances under which you may need to cancel your booking and request a refund. This policy outlines the conditions under which refunds will be provided.

2. General Conditions

Refunds will be processed based on the following general conditions:

  • Refund requests must be submitted in writing (via email or our online contact form).
  • Proof of purchase (booking confirmation) must be provided.
  • Refunds will be processed using the same payment method used for the original booking.

3. Cancellation by the Customer

Refunds for cancellations initiated by the customer will be handled as follows:

  • More than 30 days before departure: Full refund minus any non-refundable deposits or fees.
  • 15-30 days before departure: 50% refund of the total booking cost.
  • 7-14 days before departure: 25% refund of the total booking cost.
  • Less than 7 days before departure: No refund.

4. Cancellation by the Company

If we need to cancel a tour or service due to unforeseen circumstances, customers will be entitled to a full refund or the option to reschedule.

5. Non-Refundable Items

The following items are non-refundable:

  • Booking deposits
  • Travel insurance
  • Special request fees
  • Additional service charges (e.g., upgrades, customizations)

6. Force Majeure

In the event of force majeure (e.g., natural disasters, political instability, pandemics), refunds will be considered on a case-by-case basis. We may offer rescheduling or credit towards future bookings as an alternative to refunds.

7. Refund Processing Time

Refunds will be processed within 14 business days of receiving a valid refund request. The time it takes for the refund to appear in your account may vary depending on your bank or credit card issuer.
